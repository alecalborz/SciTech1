All are welcome

GMU Robotics welcomes students from all majors and academic years.
Although our team primarily consists of engineering students, we
wholeheartedly encourage involvement from anyone interested. Our
program offers a unique opportunity for students to step out of the
classroom and tackle real-world challenges, applying theoretical
knowledge in a practical context. As a result, GMU's VEX Robotics
team nurtures some of the nation's most skilled and sought-after
graduates.
